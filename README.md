Project Overview

This Digital Literacy Portfolio has been created as part of the CSE0001 course. The project focuses on developing essential digital skills required for students to effectively navigate the modern digital world.

As a Student Digital Ambassador, this project demonstrates awareness of digital tools, online platforms, professional communication, and cyber safety practices. It includes practical tasks that help build a strong digital presence and responsible online behavior.

Objectives

To understand the importance of digital literacy
To create a professional digital presence
To explore useful online platforms and tools
To practice professional communication
To raise awareness about cybercrime and online safety
