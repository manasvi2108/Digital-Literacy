Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

As a college student and Student Digital Ambassador, maintaining a responsible online presence is essential. Below is a checklist for professional and safe social media use.

Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

Social Media DO'S	Social Media DON'TS
Enable 2FA: Always use Two-Factor Authentication to secure your accounts.	Don't Share Private Info: Never post your home address, phone number, or OTPs.
Fact-Check First: Verify news and links before sharing to prevent misinformation.	Don't Engage with Trolls: Avoid responding to cyberbullies; block and report them.
Respect Privacy: Always ask for permission before tagging others in photos.	Don't Post Unprofessional Content: Avoid sharing posts that could hurt your career.
Network Professionally: Use LinkedIn to connect with industry experts in AI/ML.	Don't Click Suspicious Links: Avoid "clickbait" ads that could lead to phishing.
Build a Positive Brand: Share your academic milestones and GitHub updates.	Don't Overshare Locations: Avoid posting real-time locations for safety.
